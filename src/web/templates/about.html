About the project

This web-instrument is based an M.A. educational project on computational linguistics. The goal of the project was to analyze the Russian segment of popular science websites using tools and methods of computational linguistics.

Here you can check your text for scientiest's mentions and specific terms. Also you can identify it's scientific field and estimate readability of the text.

Our projects is specified for science-popular texts in russian.
